About Our Legacy

IEEE DTU, one of the most active student branch of IEEE proudly presents its annual technical fest TROIKA 2016. Troika, which is Russian for "a set of three", represents the three pillars of engineering: Approach, Theory and Practice.

With an array of competitive events covering all aspects of technical aptitude, Troika is one of the most eagerly awaited Techfests of North India and attracts participation from all over the country. Continuing the rich legacy, Troika 2016 aspires to be an exhibition of, and a platform for - young engineering talent.
