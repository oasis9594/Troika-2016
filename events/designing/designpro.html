Design Pro

“You can design and create, and build most wonderful place in the world but it takes people to build a reality” - Walt Disney

Do your creative wings spread and soar higher and higher when you look at an impressive poster or an amazing animation clip? Do you love to spend long hours on software like Photoshop, SolidWorks, Illustrator, InDesign etc.? If yes, then Design Pro is just for you.

Details

TROIKA 2016 comes up with an event, Design pro which is an amalgamation of photoshop, animation, solidworks, creativity and web designing. The event has five subcategories :

  • Design o thon: bring in the graphic designer within you
  • Ani –mission: gear up to showcase your animation skills
  • Web o mania: web designing challenge
  • Sketch it: unleash your creativity on paper
  • Solidworks: showcase your 3D designing skills
