Radix

Play for the robots, Play with the robots. TROIKA’16 introduces the reign of robots once again.Gather all your circuits, PCBs, Arduinos, wires and jump into the battle field of spectacular Radix’16.

Details

Radix’16 is divided in two parts, THE AUTONOMOUS EVENTS & THE MECHBOT EVENTS.

If you are well enough to analyse the black and white situations, come up with your skills and show your craft in the LFR events. It will be savage. It will be treacherous. Clash of Metals with the alloys grinding red hot. It is a game of style, control, damage and aggression with the robots pit against each other in a deadly combat.

Show up your dexterity in MECHBOT events and let the extravaganza begin! These warriors shall duel for honour and loyalty in an arena of destructions with their pneumatic spikes, flipping tusks, angle grinders, hurling maces, hydraulic pincers and lots more. Display the robustness of your bot and let it do justice when the robots collide.
